ABOUT US

KayioK Tour Safaris is a proudly Kenyan-owned tour company dedicated to offering unforgettable, authentic, and sustainable safari experiences across East Africa. Rooted in the rich heritage of the Maasai community and powered by expert safari professionals, we provide travelers with an opportunity to explore Kenya’s breathtaking landscapes, iconic wildlife, and vibrant cultures.

We specialize in tailor-made safari experiences designed to meet the unique needs of every traveler—whether you are a solo adventurer, family, student group, corporate team, or honeymoon couple.

    ✔Vision

To be a leading and trusted safari company in Kenya, offering exceptional and sustainable travel experiences that connect people with the beauty of Africa.

    ✔Mission

To deliver memorable, safe, and sustainable tour experiences through professional service, responsible tourism practices, and personalized travel solutions that exceed customer expectations.

    ✔Core value

Integrity: Honesty and transparency in all our operations.
Professionalism: High-quality service delivered by trained and experienced guides.
Sustainability: Tourism that conserves wildlife and protects the environment. Community Empowerment: Supporting local Maasai communities through employment and cultural tourism. Customer Satisfaction: Personalized itineraries and exceptional care

OUR STORY

KayioK Tour Safaris was founded with a vision to connect people from all over the world with the true spirit of Africa. Inspired by the natural beauty of Kenya and the deep cultural values of the Maasai people, our company embraces responsible tourism, community empowerment, wildlife conservation, and world-class service.

We also have diverse languages for example Spanish,English,Kiswahili and maasai Our growth is built on trust, professionalism, and the satisfaction of clients who return again and again for new adventures..
