Our Background

Who we are & What we do?

KayioK Tour Safaris is a proudly Kenyan-owned tour company dedicated to offering unforgettable, authentic, and sustainable safari experiences across East Africa. Rooted in the rich heritage of the Maasai community and powered by expert safari professionals, we provide travelers with an opportunity to explore Kenya’s breathtaking landscapes, iconic wildlife, and vibrant cultures.

We specialize in tailor-made safari experiences designed to meet the unique needs of every traveler—whether you are a solo adventurer, family, student group, corporate team, or honeymoon couple.

Vision

To be a leading and trusted safari company in Kenya, offering exceptional and sustainable travel experiences that connect people with the beauty of Africa.
